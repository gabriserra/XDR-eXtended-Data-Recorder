XDR: a flexible solution

XDR ranks as an incremental innovation in the car insurance marketplace

The project presented hereafter aims to offer a complete solution to record and evaluate driving behaviour. Most of the solutions already adopted in the market are able to deal only with a small amount of driving parameters. The proposed solution is XDR, a new in-vehicle monitoring system able to monitor and evaluate the driving behavior of the driver.
